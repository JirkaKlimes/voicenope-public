What We Do

VoiceNope (@voicenope) is an Instagram service that transcribes voice messages. Users forward voice messages to our account, and we respond with text transcripts.

Information We Collect

We collect:

  • Voice messages you send to @voicenope via Instagram Direct Messages
  • Your Instagram username (for responding to your messages)
  • Usage data (number of transcriptions) to track your credit balance
  • Payment information through Buy Me a Coffee (handled by their secure payment processor)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and transcribe your voice messages
  • Send transcripts back to you via Instagram
  • Track your usage and credit balance
  • Process payments for additional credits

Data Retention

Voice messages are processed immediately and not stored permanently. We only retain usage counts to track your credits. You can request deletion of all your data at any time.

Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Instagram's API for receiving and sending messages
  • Buy Me a Coffee for payment processing
  • Speech-to-text services for transcription

Your Rights

You can:

  • Request deletion of your data
  • Stop using the service at any time
  • Contact us with questions about your data
