Last updated: [Current Date]

Quick Summary: We only store your usage count to track credits. Voice messages are not stored permanently.

What Data We Store

VoiceNope stores minimal data about you:

  • Your Instagram username
  • Usage count (number of transcriptions used)
  • Credit balance

What We Don't Store

We do NOT permanently store:

  • Voice message audio files
  • Transcription text
  • Message history
  • Personal information beyond your Instagram username

How to Request Data Deletion

To delete all your data from VoiceNope:

  1. Send a message to @voicenope on Instagram with "DELETE MY DATA"
  2. Or email us at: [your-email] with your Instagram username

What Happens When You Request Deletion

Within 30 days, we will:

  • Delete your usage count records
  • Remove your credit balance
  • Stop processing any future messages from your account

Automatic Data Deletion

Voice messages are automatically deleted immediately after transcription. Only usage statistics are retained.

Third-Party Data

For data held by third parties (Instagram, Buy Me a Coffee), you'll need to contact them directly:

  • Instagram: Through their data deletion tools
  • Buy Me a Coffee: Through their support system
